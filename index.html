About Chikankari

Chikankari is a traditional embroidery art that originated in Lucknow, Uttar Pradesh. It is believed to have been introduced during the Mughal era and has been passed down through generations. This craft involves intricate hand embroidery on fabrics like cotton, georgette, and silk. The beauty of chekankari lies in its fine thread work, floral patterns, and elegant designs. it is widely appreciated for its comfort, simplicity, and timeless appeal.
